About

Santa Monica Physical Therapy (formerly Gail Wehner Physical Therapy) is a clinic that focuses on what matters most: the patient. We pride ourselves in providing those in our care one-on-one treatment for an hour without using any aides or PT Assistants in order to give you the best possible treatment. We will help you return to the things you love doing with a combination of manual therapy, education on how your body functions, and graded therapeutic exercises to improve the way you move.

Services

1-TO-1 HANDS-ON DIRECT CARE

  • Comprehensive evaluation
  • Patient education
  • Manual Therapy
  • Joint mobilizations
  • Soft tissue mobilizations
  • Therapeutic exercises
  • Biomechanical evaluation and training
  • Postural training
  • Functional retraining
  • Gait training
  • Balance training
  • Sports specific training
  • Workstation ergonomics
  • Running Analysis

THERAPEUTIC MODALITIES

  • Mechanical spinal traction
  • Electrical stimulation (TENS)
  • Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (NMES)
  • Ultrasound
  • Phonophoresis
  • Iontophoresis
  • Theragun
  • McConnell Tape
  • K-Tape
