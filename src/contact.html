New patients

Your first visit will primarily be an initial evaluation to determine specific impairments and to establish goals. All appointments are approximately one hour in length. Please wear comfortable clothes that can adequately expose areas for examination. Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, prescription for physical therapy, and any copies of surgical reports or imaging (MRI, CT, X-Ray). You may arrive 10 minutes early to fill out forms or fill them out ahead of time by downloading the forms here.

Appointments

Use the form below to contact us and we’ll call you back to schedule an appointment. Please be as detailed as possible. Include your insurance provider, referring physician, and diagnosis if possible. To help us best serve your inquiry, we recommend that you first describe the issue you’re having before telling us what you want to achieve. You may also call us directly to make an appointment. Our general response time is one business day.
