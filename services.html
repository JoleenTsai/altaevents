Physical Therapy

Doctors Of Physical Therapy

Specializing in orthopedic and sports conditions, both surgical and non-surgical our expert therapists provide comprehensive rehabilitation to the spine and extremities. We target impairments and functional limitations related to weakness, pain, postural dysfunction, and other medical conditions. An emphasis on evidence-based and goal-oriented interventions is placed on all aspects of a patient's treatment program.
